JOB RELATED OF COMMERCE

Accountant:

▸ Prepares and examines financial records.

▸ Ensures that financial statements are accurate and comply with laws and regulations.

Financial Analyst

▸ Analyzes financial data and trends.

▸ Provides investment recommendations.

Auditor:

▸ Reviews financial accounts and records.

▸ Ensures accuracy and compliance with regulations.

Tax Consultant:

▸ Provides advice on tax planning.

▸ Prepares tax returns for individuals or businesses.

Banker:

▸ Provides financial services to individuals and businesses.

▸ Manages financial transactions, loans, and investments.

Financial Planner:

▸ Helps clients manage their finances.

▸ Advises on investments, insurance, and retirement planning.

Management Consultant:

▸ Advises organizations on business strategies.

▸ Helps improve efficiency and profitability.

Stockbroker:

▸ Buys and sells stocks and other securities.

▸ Provides investment advice to clients.

Insurance Agent/Broker:

▸ Sells insurance policies.

▸ Advises clients on coverage and benefits.

Marketing Manager:

▸ Develops marketing strategies to promote products or services.

▸ Manages marketing campaigns and teams.

Human Resources Manager:

▸ Manages recruitment, training, and employee relations.

▸ Ensures compliance with labor laws.

Sales Manager:

▸ Leads a sales team.

▸ Develops sales strategies and sets sales targets.

Economist:

▸ Analyzes economic data.

▸ Provides insights into economic trends and forecasts.

Supply Chain Manager:

▸ Manages the flow of goods and services.

▸ Ensures efficient and cost-effective operations.

Corporate Lawyer:

▸ Specializes in business law.

▸ Advises companies on legal matters.

Real Estate Agent/Broker:

▸ Facilitates the buying, selling, and renting of properties.

▸ Advises clients on real estate transactions.

Entrepreneur:

▸ Starts and manages a new business venture.

▸ Identifies opportunities and develops business plans.

Investment Banker:

▸ Provides financial advisory services to corporations.

▸ Helps with mergers, acquisitions, and raising capital.

Business Analyst:

▸ Analyzes business processes and systems.

▸ Recommends improvements to enhance efficiency.

Credit Analyst:

▸ Evaluates creditworthiness of individuals or businesses.

▸Recommends credit terms and limits.
